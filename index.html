Features

Build Habits, Not Just Playlists

PlaylistPilot gives you the dashboard and structures needed to turn passive viewing into active learning.

Smart Daily Scheduling

Input your daily availability, and our algorithm splits videos into manageable, daily milestones that keep you moving.

Adjustable Speed Settings

Prefer watching at 1.25x or 1.5x? We calculate exact timeline completions adjusted for your preferred playback speed.

Flexible Study Revision

Toggle dedicated revision days to pause the course and review notes, ensuring you build long‑term retention.

About PlaylistPilot

I built PlaylistPilot because I kept losing track of the mountain of YouTube tutorials I wanted to finish. It felt like a graveyard of unfinished playlists. By mapping each playlist to a calendar, you finally get a roadmap to finish what you started.

Tech stack: Node.js, Express, and vanilla JavaScript.
